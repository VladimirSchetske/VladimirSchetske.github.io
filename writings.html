The mirror stage

A system of understanding demands a certain looking down on what you are trying to understand. In other words, you need to take a step back and construct a way in which things are, in order to understand it. This makes understanding how everything works inherently impossible.
In the same way a mirror does not reflect the real world perfectly, and in the same way every individual fails at describing themself (and makes the idea of self-reflection look like a joke), so also works knowledge. You cannot describe something the way it is because the construction needs time to be built. This is why recent history seems so turbulent. It needs time to settle, to become a certain way, and to find a place in relation to what came before and what comes next.
